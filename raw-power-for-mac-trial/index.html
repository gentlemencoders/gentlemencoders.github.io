RAW Power for Mac Trial (3.4.17)

Important information about the RAW Power Trial:

  • The trial has no time limit.
  • It requires macOS 10.14, 10.15, Big Sur, Monterey, Ventura, or Sonoma.
  • Extended RAW Support for Fuji Compressed RAW, GoPro, etc. requires macOS Big Sur or later. Click here for the full list
  • However, some cameras require Ventura or later. See the above list for more details.
  • The release notes can be found here:
  • It is optimized for Apple Silicon (M1)
  • It is localized in English, French, German, Spanish, Simplified Chinese and Traditional Chinese.
  • The Photos extension is included.
  • The trial is full-featured, except for the following:
    • Exports are limited in size.
    • Custom Watermarks are disabled.
    • All exports and output from the Photos extension are watermarked with a RAW Power logo.
    • Previews and thumbnails are sometimes also watermarked after editing.
  • To install the trial, open the disk image and drag the app icon to the Applications folder.
  • To remove the trial, drag the app to the trash.

If you have questions or the trial does not download properly for you, please send an email to support@gentlemencoders.com
