The Company

Gentlemen Coders® develops and distributes software for professional and enthusiast photographers. Focusing exclusively on Apple platforms, the company offers unparalleled integration with Apple’s built-in services on macOS and iOS, maximizing customers’ investment in Apple devices and services. Gentlemen Coders’ products consistently deliver high-quality image processing, innovative workflows, intuitive organizational tools and high performance. Gentlemen Coders also prides itself on exceptional customer support.

Gentlemen Coders was founded in 2016 by Nik Bhatt, an 18 year veteran of Apple. He was a member of the very small team that created TrueType in the 80s. He left Apple, working at a series of startups, before returning in the early 2000s. He then led the teams that created Soundtrack and Soundtrack Pro, before moving into Photo apps. There he served as Senior Director of Engineering and Chief Technical Officer. He led the Aperture, iPhoto, RAW Camera and Core Image engineering teams, as well as the imaging team for the Mac version of Photos.

Mr. Bhatt holds 59 patents in a wide range of disciplines including image processing, audio processing, geotagging, wireless networking, and user interface design.
