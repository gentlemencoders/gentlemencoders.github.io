You must have a purchased a copy of RAW Power (either for iOS or for Mac). Further, you must use RAW Power to edit the submitted image. Trial copies of RAW Power do not qualify. The original image can be taken in any format (RAW, JPEG, HEIF, etc.). This is a skill-based contest and chance plays no part in the determination of winners. The Contest is subject to all applicable laws and regulations and is void where prohibited.

  4. How to Enter:
    • Follow @gentlemencoders on Instagram.
    • Edit your work with RAW Power, upload it on Instagram using hashtag #rawpowercontest and tagging @gentlemencoders. (You can't participate with the trial version).
    • Tag a friend in the comments below and comment “Done”

    • The photo must be your original creation. The entrant may not use copyrighted materials from others. Photographs must be a single work of original material taken by the Entrant.

Gentlemen Coders, LLC’s decision is final. The winning entrant will be notified via DM on Instagram. If the winning entrant is unable to be contacted after reasonable attempts have been made to do so (or if the winning entrant has not complied with the contest rules), Gentlemen Coders reserves the right to offer the prize to another entry at its sole discretion. Upon notification, the winner will need to supply both the original image and a full-resolution edited image.
